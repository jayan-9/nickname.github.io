Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For weekend inquiries, you can expect a response by Monday.

Can I suggest new styles or features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Please use the "Feature Request" option in the contact form above. We review all suggestions and implement the most requested features.

Do you offer custom name style services?

Currently, we focus on providing free tools for everyone. However, for commercial or enterprise needs, please contact us with details about your project.

Can I use your tool for commercial purposes?

Yes, the names generated by our tool can be used for personal or commercial purposes. However, please ensure they don't infringe on any trademarks or intellectual property rights.

Is my data safe when I use your website?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. We don't store the names you generate on our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
